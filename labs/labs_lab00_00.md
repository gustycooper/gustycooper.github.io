## Lab Submissions on Canvas

The due dates for lab submissions are part of the Canvas lab assignments.  The points for each lab must be submitted prior to its due date.  Canvas has an assignment submission for each of the following labs.

* Intro and 1st Programs
* Expressions, Methods
* Simple Objects
* Control Flow
* Arrays, ArrayLists
* Classes, Objects, ...
* Packages, ADTs
* Graphics
* Recursion
* Searching
* Sorting
* Total Points

## Sample Lab Submission

Each lab submission requires the following.

* The UMW Honor Pledge with your name that serves as your signature.
* The name of your teammate who verfied your lab.
* The .java files that comprise your code for the labs.
* You do not have to submit your answers to the questions.
* The total points earned on the lab.
* A list showing the individual labs and the points earned.

The following demonstrates what is expected for submitting your lab completion results on Canvas.

## Total Points Lab Assignment

The Total Points Lab Assignment is where you submit the total points received on our labs.  This is a summation of the points achieved on the individual labs.  This must be submited prior the last day of class.  The total points are used to determine your lab grade.
