## Laboratory Assignments

Thursdays begin with a lecture/discussion class period followed by a lab class period with mandatory attendance.  Labs consist of programming problems and questions that focus on the material discussed prior to the lab.  You will write Java code to solve the problems. You will begin the lab during our lab class period, completing the lab during the next week.  I will begin labs by leading specific problems of the lab.  You can follow along with me or you can perform the lab at your own pace, asking questions as needed. The following are attributes of labs.

* All labs have to be completed with a grade higher than a 0 to pass the class.
* All labs must be completed by due dates, which will be on Canvas.  Because topics and course projects build on information covered in laboratory exercises, it is in your best interest to complete the labs on time.
* Labs submitted after the due date must be discussed with me for a grade.
* Labs will be self-graded, which include demonstrating your work to a team member.  Each lab contains self-grading criteria.
