## Quizzes

Quizzes are a component of our class.  Some quizzes are entirely individual.  Some quizzes consist of an individual quiz followed by a team quiz.  During the individual portion, each student answers the quiz questions by themself.  The team quiz consists of the same questions.  During the team portion, teammates collaborate on the questions to reach a consensus answer.
