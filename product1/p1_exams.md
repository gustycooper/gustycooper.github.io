## Examinations

**NO** makeup exams will be given except in the case of an unavoidable absence that can be verified as legitimate through the Office of Academic Services. In the case of a legitimate, verified, planned absence, alternate exam arrangements must be made well in advance.

The Midterm Exam is currently scheduled for Thursday, February 25.  Any changes to this schedule will be announced at least one week before the exam date.

The final exam for Section 1 will be given on Tuesday, April 26 from 0830-1100.

The final exam for Sections 2 and 3 will be given on Tuesday, April 26 from noon-2:30.
