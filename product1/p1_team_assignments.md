## Team Assignments

Team assignments will be an integral component of our class.  I will randomly group students into teams.  Team members will sit in close proximity during lectures and labs. Teams will accumulate points.  At the end of the semester, the team grade will be determined by total points earned divided by the total points available.  Team assignments will include bonus points, which allows the team grade to be greater than 100.  Each member of a team will receive the same grade.  The following are examples of team assignments.

* Short, multiple-choice team quizzes as part of lectures.  
* Team components of our programming projects.  
* Team components of our labs.
