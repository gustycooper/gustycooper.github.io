## CPSC 220 Course Agreement

My signature below acknowledges that I have read and understood the material in the syllabus for CPSC 220. I also acknowledge the following.

1. I have successfully completed the course prerequisite CPSC 110 or have equivalent AP/Transfer Credit or received instructor/chair permission to register for this course based on prior experience.

2. I understand that in order to be considered for a passing grade:
   * I must complete all laboratory assignments with at least 1 point prior to the last day of classes.
   * I must take all tests on the announced dates.
   * I must have a passing average (60% or better) in the test component.
   * I must have a passing average (60% or better) in the programming component of the course.

3. I understand the criteria for program and laboratory assignment evaluation as explained in the syllabus. I understand that all labs must be verified as complete prior to the last day of class in order for me to pass the course.

4. I am expected to attend class during every scheduled class meeting. I am responsible for attending lab every week. I am responsible for all assigned readings and all materials presented in class lectures. I am responsible for obtaining all materials and notes for any classes for which I am absent or arrive late.

5. I am aware of Gusty’s office hours. I understand that if I need to assistance from Gusty, but cannot meet during scheduled office hours, I may email Gusty for assistance or to schedule an appointment.   

6. I have read and understand the honor code and information about how it applies to all assignments in this course.

7. I understand that Canvas is our primary source of communication and I should read Canvas daily.

8. I understand that to achieve the highest level of success in this course, I should regularly attend class, actively listen and take notes during lecture, ask for assistance in a timely manner when topics are unclear to me, begin all assignments on the day they are assigned, and review any errors that I make on assignments after the graded assignment has been returned.

9. Gusty will provide class presentations and materials pertinent to the course. Gusty will distribute all assignments in a manner that permits adequate time for students to complete then. Gusty will be available during posted office hours (unless otherwise announced) and provide assistance via email as well. Gusty will grade and return all tests and assignments in a timely fashion.
