## Responsibilities and Attendance

You are expected to attend all classes and labs.  You are responsible for understanding the material in lectures, labs, projects, handouts, reading assignments, and Canvas postings.  You are responsible for understanding our class schedule, including schedule changes made in class and via Canvas.  If you miss a class, you are responsible for making up the work.  Our labs are in Trinkle B12, which has UMW computers; however, I suggest using personal laptops for labs because is simplifies moving data.  Some exercises during lectures will require a laptop.  For example, we may have team programming assignment or begin a lab early.  You are responsible for bringing your laptop with enough charge to accomplish in class assignments, including assignments on Thursdays that have a lecture and a lab.  Classrooms are not equipped with many power receptacles.
