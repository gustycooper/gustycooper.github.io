# Students with Disabilities

The Office of Disability Services has been designated by the University of Mary Washington as the primary office to guide, counsel, and assist students with disabilities. If you receive services through this office and require accommodations for this class, please schedule an appointment with me as soon as possible to discuss your approved accommodation needs.  Bring your accommodation letter with you.  I will hold all information in the strictest confidence unless you give me permission to do otherwise.
